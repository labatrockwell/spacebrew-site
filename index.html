/Launching Spacebrew

Spacebrew can be launched in two ways.

1. Online via our shared. hosted server.

With this public option, you will see any publishers. subscribers, and routes that other users have established. You will have the ability to route new connections and re-route existing ones. Users will also be able to route to and from your publishers and subscribers. If no other users are connected. the admin site will appear blank

Spacebrew Cloud

2. Downloaded and run locally.

This method requires a bit more setup, but will launch a private session wherever it in installed. Visit our wiki for more detailed setup instructions.
